SITE PURPOSE

The purpose of this site is to provide users with a pleasant experience when consulting a website on the weather, topics of interest, images, recreational information, among other things, from three different locations.
To achieve this, the site will support responsive design techniques to allow a good distribution of the site and its content on all kinds of devices (laptops, tablets, cell phones).
