TARGETED AUDIENCE

The target audience of mumterra.com website is described below:

Who:
All kinds of people, women and men, young and old, who are looking for recreational options, or want to know what the weather would be like in the next days.

Age:
10 to 90.

Technology:
People don't need to have a lot of experience with technology, just the basic knowledge of how to navigate a website.

Income:
Income levels are usually low. It is generally the middle class who reads this site.

MACY RAWLINGS

-Occupation:
Part time job at a call center.
-Demographics and education:
She is currently in college, but works part time. She is 22 years old.
-Goals and motivation to use the site:
She recently moved to one of the locations where the mumterra.com site provides updates on the weather and other topics of interest. Since she knows almost no place or person, she is looking for the best option to go out and have fun and make new friends through the web.
-Social:
Although she is very serious, she has a gift for making friends. She likes to cook, but does not like sports, and is very dedicated to her studies.
-Technology:
She uses her phone and her computer only to do her homework. In general, she does not like social networks and prefers to take photos as a hobby in her spare time.
-Quote:
"Give people what they want"

ISAAC LUGO

-Occupation:
He owns a medium-sized bakery.
-Demographics and education:
57 years old, he did not finish university, but he always likes to learn something new. Everyone considers him as the best chef in all the town.
-Objectives and motivation to use the site:
It has no specific purpose, you only check the web because you like to know what is happening and what is new in your city. From time to time you find weather predictions helpful.
-Social:
He is a very nice person, always kind to his clients. He has a great facility for manual work and for being sociable.
-Technology:
He does not have a computer or telephone, but he visits the website when hIS grandchildren are going to visit her and they put the website on his cell phone. Isaac uses this app just for pleasure and to check how the weather will be in the last week.
-Quote:
"Jugar o ser jugado"
